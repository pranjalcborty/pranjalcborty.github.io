About Me

I am currently finishing up my Masters in Computer Science at Brock University and working as a graduate teaching and research assistant. My research tries to solve memory related issues in resource constrained systems, under the supervision of Dr. Naser Ezzati-Jivan. Before my masters journey, I had worked as a software engineer at Therap for more than four years, where I was involved in developing and maintaining modules for an enterprise web application.
