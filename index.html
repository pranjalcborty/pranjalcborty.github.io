About Me

I am working as a Programmer and Systems Analyst at Brock University Library. Before starting here, I completed my Masters at Brock, where my research tried to solve memory related issues in resource constrained systems, under the supervision of Dr. Naser Ezzati-Jivan. Before my masters journey, I had worked as a software engineer at Therap for more than four years, where I was involved in developing and maintaining modules for an enterprise web application.
